About

We are four master's students in Information Management and Systems, Class of 2013 at the School of Information at UC Berkeley. This project is completed as our master's final project. We worked together from December, 2012 to May, 2013. The project information, including the project report, can also be accessed here.

We are also pleased to have Proteus Digital Health as our project sponsor. Building on their ingestible sensor in the medicine and wearable patch, our app is able to remind the patient to take her pills, or nudge her to go for a walk when it's nice outside.
